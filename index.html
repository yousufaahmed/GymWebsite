Welcome to Health and Leisure centre!

About

We are a Health and Leisure centre located in Kesgrave, Ipswich and we pride ourselves in having the best facilities and customer service. Our facilities include a fully functioning gym, a swimming pool, a tennis court, a football court, and a variety of other beautiful facilities. We have a few hand picked, community loved restaurants, and an internet cafe to keep you full, awake and hydrated. Once you walk through our doors, you will be in love with our centre, and you most probably will never leave, unless we are closed of course!
